Favorite Artwork and Story

I was not entirely clear on the instruction for this part so I thought I'd share a story of my own and some of the photos related to it. A favorite story of mine, with images attached would be in the summer of 2024. I have never been a morning person however, I found out that some of the best memories come from uncomfortable moments. In early June I was camping with a few of my friends in the Tetons and one morning there we woke up at around 5 in the morning to drive about half an hour to the Jackson Lake Dam and it was beyond magnificent. There was a layer of fog on the water and the sun was just peaking up behind the distant mountains. I was so elated because of the photography opportunities it provided, because of this, I have attached a few to this page. I would personally count these photos as artworks so I hope they count!
